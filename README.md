## 🌐 About ProdXCloud

**ProdXCloud** (also known as **VxCloud**) is an enterprise-grade, AI-powered multi-cloud DevOps platform built for engineering teams who demand speed, reliability, and security at scale.

We combine **Git-native workflows**, **Infrastructure as Code**, **policy automation**, **observability**, and **agentic AI** to make cloud operations faster, safer, and context-aware — across **8+ cloud providers** and **30+ global regions**.

Whether you are a startup shipping your first product or an enterprise managing thousands of microservices, ProdXCloud gives you the tools, automation, and intelligence to operate at the highest level.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Connect Git** — Authorize GitHub and/or GitLab for repositories and pipeline runners
2. **Connect clouds** — Add AWS, Azure, GCP, and other accounts with least-privilege roles
3. **Select a blueprint** — Choose from application, data, or platform foundation blueprints
4. **Provision** — Launch environments via pipeline or UI with automated policy enforcement
5. **Deploy** — Use GitOps to promote changes through environments with approvals and automated checks
